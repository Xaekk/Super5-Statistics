Services and Treatments

The services offered are comprehensive, ranging from simple blood tests to diagnostic scans, from cosmetic surgery to dental services, from hair removal by laser to complex cardiac surgery - all available with state-of-the-art equipment.

Some of the common interventions requested and performed include total knee replacement surgery, bilateral knee replacement, uni-compartmental knee replacement, hip replacement and hip resurfacing.

Some private hospitals offer a comprehensive range of cardiac services from out-patient consultations to angiography, angioplasty, pacemaker insertion and open-heart surgery.

Amongst the latest high-tech equipment available on the island is a top range dedicated ophthalmic unit where sutureless cataract surgery and refractive procedures are performed daily.

Specialised cosmetic surgery procedure demands continue to grow and a result, a substantial number of dedicated cosmetic surgery and beauty clinics have opened. The medical treatments offered include face lifts, otoplasty (prominent ear correction), rhinoplasty (cosmetic nose reshaping), abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), liposuction, breast augmentation, breast reduction and other cosmetic interventions.
