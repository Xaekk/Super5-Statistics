THE GREAT SPRING HORTICULTURAL SHOW

06/05/2017 - 07/05/2017

A festival of floral arrangements, plants, fruit, vegetables and crafts in a historic garden setting organized by the Malta Horticulture Society. This annual traditional show includes also a programme of dancing exhibitions, folk music  and various demonstrations.

The show is open on Saturday 6th May from 14.00hrs till 22.00hrs and on Sunday 7th May from 08.00hrs till 21.00hrs.
