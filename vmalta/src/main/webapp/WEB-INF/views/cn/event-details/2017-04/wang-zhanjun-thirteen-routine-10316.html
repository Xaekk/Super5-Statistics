WANG ZHANJUN THIRTEEN ROUTINE

Venue: Mediterranean Regional Centre for Traditional Chinese Medicine, Kordin, Paola

Applicable: people who have some knowledge of Tai Chi, WANG Zhanjun Tai Chi enthusiasts and Tai Chi instructors

Background Information

In 2015, China Cultural Centre in Malta (CCC) invited the renowned Chinese Tai Chi Master WANG Zhanjun, who set the Guinness Book World Record in 2008 for tossing a 2kg weight 98cm with his abdomen, to participate in World Tai Chi Day in Malta and conduct a " Wang Zhanjun Three Routine - Tai Chi & Push Hand" for local Tai Chi enthusiasts and professional.

Since then, CCC has invited a number of highly accomplished instructors of the Wang Zhanjun Tai Chi Comprehensive School, who are held in high esteem for both their performance and teaching expertise by prestigious Boards and Organizations in China and abroad. In response to increasing awareness of the multiple health benefits derived from regular Wang Zhanjun Tai Chi practice, and the growing anticipation and demand from local Taichi enthusiasts, CCC strives to continue providing such training courses, by which all interested can deepen their knowledge and experience of Taichi, as well as pursue a healthier lifestyle.

As continuation to the Tai Chi & Push Hand training course presented by WANG Zhanjun, based on "Wang Zhanjun Six Routine" previously held in Malta, CCC will invite Mr. LIANG Kun, an excellent Tai Chi inheritor from China, to hold "Wang Zhanjun Thirteen Routine" Tai Chi training course from 10-28 April, 2017.

Mr. LIANG Kun was born in Zhoukou City, Henan Province, in 1994. He commenced study and training under the eminent Master WANG Zhanjun in 2008, establishing a sound foundation in the essentials of Chen Tai Chi forms and national competition routines, attaining all round excellence in terms of styles, principles, techniques, theory and applications. Mr. LIANG Kun is a dedicated inheritor of the Wang Zhanjun Taichi culture and first-grade instructor in this discipline who has conducted training courses under the Wang Zhanjun Taichi brand in China and overseas. Moreover, he has been conferred several prestigious awards and championship titles, including:

2009 Jiaozuo International Taijiquan Annual Conference - Chen Style Tai Chi Quan Lao Jia Yilu First Prize

2009 Wenxian Taijiquan Sword Push Hands Championship - Chen Style Tai Chi Quan Lao Jia Yilu First Prize ; Push Hands Competition (56 kg max weight) First Prize

2010 Wenxian Taijiquan Sword Push Hands Championship - Chen Style Tai Chi Quan Lao Jia Yilu First Prize

2010 Henan Traditional Martial Arts Tournament - Tai Chi Spear Form First Prize

2011 Jiaozuo International Taijiquan Annual Conference - Chen Style Tai Chi Quan Lao Jia Yilu First Prize ; Tai Chi Spear Form First Prize

2012 Henan traditional Martial Arts Tournament - Tai Chi Spear Form First Prize; Chen Style Tai Chi Quan Lao Jia Yilu First Prize

2013 Jiaozuo International Taijiquan Exchange Competition - Tai Chi Spear Form First Prize

2013 Jiaozuo International Taijiquan Annual Conference - Tai Chi Spear Form First Prize

2014 Shandong Zibo Wuyang Lake International Push Tournament - Push Hands (70 Kg) Championship Title

2015 ZhengYang (International) Taijiquan Classic Competition - Push Hands (75 Kg) Championship Title
