GĦANAFEST

28/06/2017 - 29/06/2017

As from 2017, Għanafest will make a return to its roots and the focus will be more on the local folk aspect, although it will continue to feature international folk acts too. Għanafest offers a very important platform for Malta's folk singers and musicians and, to emphasis more the ideal setting for such a musical celebration Artistic Director Renzo Spiteri and Arts Council Malta saw that it is only fitting to celebrate this by staging this festival in the picturesque surroundings of Buskett Gardens. Our local għana singers who will expose all different forms of local folk song, Maltese singer/songwriters embracing a more modern approach to Maltese singing and acts from around the Mediterranean basin will feature over a two-day event. The festival will also feature local produce, an artisan market and a children's area that will certainly make it a great day out for the whole family. All this is happening on the 28th June, eve of Imnarja, and the 29th which is a national holiday celebrating the feast of saints Paul and Peter.

For more information about Għanafest visit http://www.festivalsmalta.com.
