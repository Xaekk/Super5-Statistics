Advertising on Visitmalta

Visitmalta.com is the official tourism portal for Malta, Gozo and Comino.

Launched in 2012, the portal provides information about every aspect of the Maltese Islands as a holiday destination. It also features a comprehensive events calendar and listings of travel-related products and services.

The Visitmalta.com travel portal is currently available in the following languages: English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Swedish, Russian, Japanese, Hungarian, Turkish and Chinese. Other languages will be added in due course.

Today, Visitmalta.com is one of the most visited travel websites for Malta, attracting an average of more than 100,000 unique visitors monthly. Many of these are people who are interested in coming to Malta for their holidays.

In addition, the Visitmalta.com website ranks highly on all major search engines including Google, Yahoo, Bing, AOL, Ask and others for keywords such as ‘Malta' and ‘Malta Travel'.

Although our visitor statistics are impressive, we are constantly working to improve the site's performance and attract an increased volume of traffic.

You too can benefit from the popularity of Visitmalta.com by advertising on our portal. You can place your advert on one or more pages in one or more of the language versions. (Minimum campaign duration is just 1 month).

Rates and applicable Terms & Conditions are outlined below, where you can also find a link to the Booking Form that you can use to reserve advertising space on visitmalta.com.

For more information, or if you wish to discuss your specific requirements, please send an email to advertise@visitmalta.com or call us on 356 22915118.

ONSITE ADVERTISING - TERMS & CONDITIONS

POSITION
The space allocated for advertising consists of 1 or 2 positions located on the left-hand side and/or right-hand side of the page (depending on design layout).

This space is available on practically every page of the site, except for a few pages where it has been decided not to include advertising. MTA reserves the right to change this policy at any time, at its discretion.

SIZE
The dimensions of every advert will be 248 x 148 pixels.

FORMAT
The image must be JPEG or GIF. Animated banners are acceptable (maximum 3 rotations).
No Flash banners will be accepted.

APPROVAL
Design (text and image) is subject to approval by MTA.

ROTATION
There can be up to 3 adverts occupying the same position on a page.
If more than one advert is occupying the same slot, one of them will appear at random each time the page is viewed or refreshed.

RATES
Advertising Rates vary according to campaign duration. Minimum order is for 1 month. See below.

There are frequency discounts built into the rate card. These range from 10% for a 3 month commitment, going up to 25% for a 12 month order.

ARTWORK
Customers must provide their own artwork, at least 3 working days before the start of the campaign.

PAYMENT
Amount due is to be paid in full prior to the start of the campaign.
When a previously occupied position becomes free, preference is given to existing advertisers.

BOOKING FORM
To reserve space on visitmalta.com, please use the ADVERTISING BOOKING FORM. Upon receipt, we will get in touch with you to confirm availabiltiy and finalise booking.

CONTACT
For more information contact: advertise@visitmalta.com
