宿营

The main camping site in Malta is located at Dahlet ix-Xmajjar, near the White Tower, l/o Armier (Mellieha). The Malta Campsite site is situated approximately 30m from the sea, and 5 minutes walk to the nearest sandy beach. The site occupies an area of approximately 12 tumoli, and offers a range of facilities including a swimming pool.

The Malta Campsite incorporates a large area for pitching your own tent(s), touring caravans and RV area. The site is surrounded by the beautiful Maltese landscape and breath-taking crystal-clear Mediterranean Sea.

The Malta Campsite is fully enclosed, security is provided 24 hours per day. Tents are available for hire from March to October (Furnished). Mobile homes are available for purchase and hire. Plots are available on annual contract.

For more information visit our website http://www.maltacampsite.com/
