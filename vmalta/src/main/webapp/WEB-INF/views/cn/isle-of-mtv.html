Isle of MTV

The Isle of MTV is an annual music festival organized by MTV Europe, which is part of Viacom Media Networks, in collaboration with local partners G7 Events and with the support of the Malta Tourism Authority. It has been held in Malta since 2007 and is now well established as one of the biggest music festivals in Europe, attracting crowds of more than 50,000 people.
