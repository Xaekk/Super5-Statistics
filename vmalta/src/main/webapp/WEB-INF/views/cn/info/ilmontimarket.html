Merchants Street, Valletta, Malta

The Merchant Street Market, open every day from 8:00 am until noon, is a popular open-air market in Valletta. This colorful street market resembles a European flea market, offering everyday and brand name clothing, antiques, old books and many other items at bargain prices.
