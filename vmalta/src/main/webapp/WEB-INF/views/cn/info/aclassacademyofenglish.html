A CLASS ACADEMY OF ENGLISH

St. Catherine's High School, 11, Alamein Road Pembroke, PBK 1771, Malta

AClass Academy of English is Malta's friendliest English Language school. We form part of St Catherine's High School (http://www.stcatherines.eu ) which was established in 1909 and has a solid reputation for academic excellence on the island.

We have 8 bright and airy classrooms and offer courses for General English, Business English, Medical English, Aviation English and Exam Preparation Courses. Small classes and native English teachers in a relaxed but professional environment. Individuals and Groups are welcome. Prices starting from as little as €95 per week. Visit our website http://www.aclassenglish.com or send us an email to info@aclassenglish.com  for more information. You can also find us on Facebook.
