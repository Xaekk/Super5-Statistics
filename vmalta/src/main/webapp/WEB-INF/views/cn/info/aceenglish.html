ACE ENGLISH MALTA

Bay Street Complex, Level 4, St. George's Bay, St. Julian’s, STJ 3311, Malta

ACE English Malta provides a fulfilling education to students who are interested in achieving specific language objectives. ACE English Malta is a new breed of a dynamic institution focused on offering specialized courses to meet the demands of students in the 21st Century. Vibrant interiors, very modern and chic surroundings, 26 modern classrooms technology all equipped with interactive whiteboards, chill out areas, huge outdoor terrace, free Wi-Fi and Internet.
