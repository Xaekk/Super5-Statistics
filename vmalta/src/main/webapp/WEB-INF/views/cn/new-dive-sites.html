New Dive Sites

In addition to the various existing dive sites that can be found around the Maltese Islands, the Malta Tourism Authority actively contributes to the creation of new dive sites. This is done by means of the scuttling of vessels onto a sandy patch of seabed, which eventually become artifical reefs.

The latest such initiative saw the scuttling of an old tug boat off Exiles, in Sliema, on 20th June 2013. 

The vessel, cleaned of all oil and grease, was sunk onto the seabed 20 metres below the surface some 250 metres off St Julians and 389 metres from Portomaso breakwater in a project launched by the Professional Diving Schools Association in collaboration with the Malta Tourism Authority as part of the maltagoesrural initiative.
