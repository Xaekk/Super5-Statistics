当地旅行社

Local travel agents have the knowledge and experience to help you get the best from your holiday in Malta and Gozo. Some of the companies listed on this site are official representatives of foreign tour operators, and work in close cooperation with them to develop the perfect itineraries for their clients. Others specialise in a particular activity such as pilgrim tours, weddings and honeymoons or the organisation of conferences and incentives. All are licensed by the Malta Tourism Authority.
