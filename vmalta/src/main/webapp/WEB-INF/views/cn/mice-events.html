会展活动

Throughout the year, the Malta Tourism Authority organises or takes part in a number of events which are of interest to the MICE segment.

 

IMEX 2014
20-22 May 2014
Frankfurt - Germany
Malta Stand D 120

At this year's IMEX fair, Europe's foremost forum for the MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Events) sector, the Malta Tourism Authority shall be welcoming visitors to its 145 square metre stand, which can be found at number D 120 in the IMEX exhibition hall, in Frankfurt, Germany. The MTA and its trade partners will be highlighting the many advantages of Malta and Gozo as destinations for the Meetings Industry, and offering genuine local hospitality at the stand.

MICE Business Travel Market 2013
19-20 February 2013
Event-Arena, Olympiapark Munich - Germany
Stand no. N41

Malta is participating in the first edition of MICE Business Travel Market 2013 in Munich in February. The new trade fair, which will be held on the 19th and 20th of February at Munich’s Olympic Park, is the first to address the trend towards consolidation of the business travel and corporate events segments on one single platform. The fair is the only German trade fair for organisers of events and business travel and has been met with an encouraging response from travel professionals.
