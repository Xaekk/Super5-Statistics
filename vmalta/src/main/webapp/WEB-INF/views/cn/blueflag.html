Blue Flag

Malta now boasts of ten Blue Flag Beaches, and these are: St. George's Bay, Fond Għadir Bay, Westin Dragonara Resort, Qawra Point, Buġibba Perched Beach, Għajn Tuffieħa, Għadira Bay, Paradise Bay, Golden Bay and Ramla Bay. Another two beaches in Gozo achieved a Beach of Quality status, namely Ħondoq Bay and Marsalforn Bay.
