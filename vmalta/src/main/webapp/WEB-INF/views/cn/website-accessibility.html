Website Accessibility

马耳他旅游局根据不同人群对系统的不同需求，开发出了VisitMalta门户网站，尽可能地吸引更多的访客。我们尽一切努力确保本门户网站符合网站标准化管理（3.0版）的易访性要求和建议，并确保特定的残障人士能够轻松地访问本网站。

屏幕阅读器用户：如果屏幕阅读器用户在使用扬声器插件时用的是Window-Eyes V7.2或以上版本时，应切换至浏览模式关（CTRL SHIFT A），以便能够有效利用音频播放器的按钮。

如何使用语音朗读功能ReadSpeaker

 

ReadSpeaker可以朗读网站上的文字。如果您不方便阅读在线文本，这款朗读软件可以为您提供帮助，让您更加容易理解网站上的信息。通过ReadSpeaker，您可以更加方便、舒适地访问我们的网站，同时无需要下载任何东西。

How does it work?

Listen to the main content
You simply click the listen button to listen to the web page's content:


A control panel will appear (see image below) and ReadSpeaker automatically starts reading the text of the web page out loud.


In the control panel you can:
• Adjust the volume
• Pause the reading
• Stop the reading
• Restart the reading
• Choose "No sound?" if your browser does not support flash
• Choose "Settings" if you want to customize the behaviour of the service

Listen to selected text
You can select a part of the text that you want to listen to and then press the pop-up listen button which appears next to the mouse pointer. When you click the pop-up listen button, a smaller control panel will appear (see the image below) and ReadSpeaker will automatically start reading the text you have selected.


In this control panel you can:
• Pause the reading
• Stop the reading
• Restart the reading
• Customize the behaviour of the service by clicking on the wrench icon

Settings
In the settings menu you can:
• Choose whether you want to have the text highlighted word by word and/or sentence by sentence, or turn off the highlighting
• Choose whether you want the pop up listen button to be shown when text is selected
• Choose whether you want the reading speed to be slow, medium or fast

 



If you click on the flash player it will be in focus so that you can control it with the keyboard:
• Play/pause: Space bar
• Stop: Esc key
• Increase volume: Up arrow or the key of the numeric keypad
• Lower Volume: Down arrow or the - key of the numeric keypad
• Fast forward: Right arrow
• Rewind: Left arrow

For more information please visit ReadSpeaker's text to speech online site.
